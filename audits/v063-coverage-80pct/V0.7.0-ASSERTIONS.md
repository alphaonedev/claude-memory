## Summary

**v0.6.3 ships at 93.05% line coverage** with 1809+ tests (1578 lib + 11 bin + 210 integration + 10 SAL contract). Every module except `reranker.rs` (79.25%, neural-gated) is ≥91%; three modules at 100% (`main.rs`, `errors.rs`, `color.rs`).

**The 6 pp gap to a hypothetical 99%** is roughly:
- 2 pp infrastructure-blocked (HF-Hub for reranker neural + embeddings local)
- 2 pp federation/handlers fanout match arms (W13 territory; needs ~50 mock-peer-extension tests)
- 1 pp `mcp.rs` LLM-dependent paths (needs MockOllama wired into mcp::tests)
- 1 pp structurally-unreachable code (Arc::try_unwrap, join-errors, mutex-poison, 100k eviction)

The 4 architectural debts (D1–D5) and 7 test-infrastructure items (C1–C7) are the v0.7.0 quality program. The 3 deferred bugs (B1–B3) are small fixes that can land as their own ~30-line PRs early in v0.7.0.

**No outstanding security defects.** The 2 SSRF gaps discovered in v0.6.3 W10 by Closer L10b were fixed in W11 (commit `9eeb453`, Option 2 approved by operator); the corresponding `#[ignore]` tests were un-ignored and pass cleanly.
